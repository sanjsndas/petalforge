Contact PetalForge

Welcome to PetalForge’s Contact page. We deeply value your interest, questions, and feedback. Connecting with our clients and community is a cornerstone of our philosophy and success. Visit Our Atelier

Our studio is a peaceful retreat in Brooklyn, where we craft unique floral creations inspired by nature’s beauty. We invite you to visit to see our latest displays and have a firsthand conversation with our floral artisans.

We also welcome visits for consultations and workshop registration. Prior appointment booking is recommended to ensure personalized attention.

Frequently Asked Questions

We anticipate your questions! Here are some detailed answers to frequently asked inquiries.

What floral services does PetalForge offer?

PetalForge specializes in handcrafted floral arrangements personalized for weddings, corporate events, seasonal subscriptions, and everyday beauty. We also provide expert pruning and plant care consultations.

How can I schedule a workshop?

Workshops are scheduled monthly. You can inquire and register via our contact form, email, or phone. Group bookings and private sessions are available.

Do you deliver flowers?

Yes, we provide local delivery within Brooklyn and surrounding areas, ensuring freshness and presentation quality upon arrival.

Can I customize my bouquet online?

Absolutely! Use the custom bouquet builder on our homepage to create your personalized floral design.

What safety measures are in place for customer interaction during COVID-19?

PetalForge maintains strict sanitation, staff health checks, and social distancing policies to ensure a safe environment for all visitors and customers.
