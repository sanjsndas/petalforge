PetalForge Cookie Policy and Detailed Information

At PetalForge, we respect your privacy and are committed to transparency regarding how we use cookies and related technologies on our website. Below you will find an extensive, detailed explanation of cookie practices and related contact points, designed to inform you about each aspect in depth.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit websites. They help websites remember your actions and preferences over time, enabling smoother and more personalized browsing experiences.

Without cookies, you may have to re-enter information every time you visit a site, resulting in a less efficient experience. PetalForge uses cookies responsibly to enhance your visits.

2. Types of Cookies Used by PetalForge

We use various categories of cookies:

  • Essential Cookies: These are required for basic website functions like page navigation and access to secure areas.
  • Performance Cookies: Collect information on website usage to improve performance.
  • Functionality Cookies: Remember user preferences to offer enhanced features.
  • Targeting and Advertising Cookies: Deliver relevant promotional content and ads.

Each category serves a specific purpose; understanding them helps you control your privacy.

3. First-Party vs Third-Party Cookies

First-party cookies are set by PetalForge's domain and only accessible by our site.

Third-party cookies arise from external services we use, such as analytics or advertising partners, which help us understand usage trends or show personalized ads.

You can control third-party cookies via your browser and consent options.

4. Cookie Consent Dialogue

We display a cookie consent banner/dialogue allowing you to accept or decline certain cookie categories. Your decision is respected and stored securely.

You can revisit or change your preferences anytime by using links in our footer.

5. How to Manage Cookies Using Your Browser

Your browser provides settings to manage cookies including blocking, deleting, or allowing them by site.

Instructions vary but generally located under privacy or security settings.

Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality.

6. Cookies Used for Analytics and Performance

We use analytics cookies to measure user engagement, page visits, click behavior, and bounce rates to improve our website design and content.

These cookies are anonymous and help us understand how visitors interact without identifying individuals.

7. Advertising Cookies and User Profiling

Advertising cookies track user interests and browsing habits to deliver personalized advertisements that match your preferences.

We work with trusted third-party networks who comply with regulations regarding consent and data protection.

8. Security and Cookies

Cookies do not contain personal information and cannot install malware or viruses on your device.

We take all possible measures to ensure cookies used on PetalForge are completely safe and secure.

9. Cookie Expiration

Cookies have varying lifespans depending on their purpose—session cookies expire when you close your browser, whereas persistent cookies remain until expiration.

PetalForge reviews cookie lifetimes regularly to optimize user experience.

10. Impact of Declining Cookies

If you decline non-essential cookies, some website functionalities such as personalized recommendations or saved preferences might be reduced or unavailable.

Essential functions will still operate properly.

11. Cookies for User Authentication

We use cookies to maintain user login sessions, so you remain authenticated across pages without having to log in repeatedly.

This improves usability and security by recognizing authenticated sessions safely.

12. Customizing Cookie Preferences

Our cookie consent dialog allows granular choices, letting you accept all, decline all, or select specific categories to suit your privacy comfort.

Your preferences are saved for future visits.

13. Cookies and Data Sharing

Cookies may allow limited sharing of aggregated, anonymized user interaction data with service providers to improve our offerings.

We never share personal identifying information via cookies.

14. Compliance with Laws

We comply with GDPR, CCPA, and other privacy laws by obtaining consent and providing transparent information about cookies and tracking.

15. Cookies on Mobile Devices

Cookies function similarly on mobile browsers and help optimize your experience on all device types.

Mobile device settings also control cookie behavior.

16. Cookie Audits
