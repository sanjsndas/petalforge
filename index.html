Expert Tips & Tricks

Enhance your floral skills with some essential tips from PetalForge artisans:

  • Choose seasonal flowers for optimal freshness and eco-friendly arrangements. Seasonality ensures blooms at their peak with the least environmental impact.
  • Use clean, sharp tools for pruning to avoid damage and promote healthy growth. Regularly sterilize your tools to prevent disease spread between plants.
  • Layer flowers by height and texture to add depth and visual interest. Start with anchoring greens and build focal flowers into the design.
  • Change water daily and trim stems at a diagonal for longer vase life. This increases water absorption and keeps flowers hydrated.
  • Mix focal flowers with fillers to achieve balanced and harmonious bouquets. This adds texture and softness, highlighting main blooms.
  • Know your flower care needs: Different flowers require specific light, water, and temperature conditions‚Äîtailor care accordingly.
  • Prune roses properly: Cut just above an outward-facing bud and remove any dead or crossing branches for healthy shape.
  • Keep foliage out of water to reduce bacterial growth in your vase and extend flower freshness.

Remember, every flower tells a story ‚Äî arrange yours with intention and care.
