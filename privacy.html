Privacy Policy

Welcome to PetalForge. We are committed to safeguarding and preserving your privacy when you visit our website or use our services. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal data you provide to us voluntarily such as your name, email, address, phone number, and payment details when you interact with our services or contact us.

Also, we collect technical and usage data automatically such as your IP address, browser type, pages viewed, and usage patterns to improve and personalize your experience.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your information is used to deliver and improve our services, communicate with you, process transactions securely, and comply with legal requirements. We also use data to tailor offers, send newsletters, and enhance user experience.

3. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data under your consent, contractual necessity, legitimate interests, or to comply with legal obligations.

4. Disclosure of Your Information

We do not sell your information. We may share data with trusted service providers for operational purposes, legal compliance, or business transfers.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies to facilitate functionality, analyze site performance, and provide personalized content. You can manage cookie preferences via your browser settings.

6. Data Security

We implement robust security measures including encryption and access controls to protect your data. Despite best efforts, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights

You have rights regarding your personal data including access, correction, deletion, and objection. You can exercise these rights by contacting us at privacy@petalforge.com.

8. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes stated or as required by law.

9. Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect data from children under 16. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child’s data is collected, please contact us to delete it.

10. International Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries with differing privacy laws. We ensure appropriate safeguards for such transfers.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in law or practice. Updates will be posted here with a revised date.

12. How to Contact Us

Detailed Policy Information

This policy covers a range of topics including the types of personal data collected, detailed reasons and legal bases for data processing, obligations of third-party service providers, data transfer protocols, cookie management recommendations, and how you can exercise your data protection rights.

Our commitment extends to ensuring transparency about how your information is handled and facilitating easy access to your data.

We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about our practices and ensure your choices and preferences are respected.

We value your trust and are devoted to protecting your privacy with the highest standards.

Below is an extended presentation of our privacy practices to help you understand the nuances and protections in place:

Data Minimization: We limit data collection to only what is necessary for legitimate business purposes, avoiding excessive collection of your personal data.

Data Access Controls: Access to your personal data is strictly controlled through role-based permissions and authentication mechanisms.

Data Subject Rights: You have rights to obtain a copy of your personal data, request corrections, object to processing, and seek deletion where applicable.

Data Breach Procedures: We have comprehensive breach detection and response protocols to promptly address any unauthorized data access.

Third-party Partners: All third-party service providers are vetted for compliance with data protection regulations, and contracts include data protection requirements.

Cookie Policy: Detailed descriptions of cookies used and how to opt out or manage your cookie preferences.

Privacy Shield Compliance: Where applicable, we comply with frameworks ensuring data protection for transfers outside your country.

We encourage you to reach out with any questions or concerns related to privacy, data security, and your rights as a user of PetalForge.
